# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors must communicate professionally, welcome good-faith questions,
credit sources, distinguish evidence from opinion, and critique work without
attacking people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and publishing
private location data are not tolerated.

## Scientific integrity

Do not fabricate benchmarks, case studies, citations, affiliations, or user
testimonials. Declare important methodological limitations and conflicts of
interest. Use anonymized or synthetic examples when real spatial data could
identify people, homes, endangered species, or protected infrastructure.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through
GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation,
or ban repeat offenders. Enforcement decisions should be documented privately
and made consistently.

This policy is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for a
scientific Agent Skills project.
